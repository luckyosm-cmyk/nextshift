# NextShift Product Requirements Document

## Product Vision

NextShift is a modern mobile application designed for people who work rotating or irregular shifts.

The goal is to become the most intuitive and complete shift management app available.

Instead of being just another shift calendar, NextShift helps users organize their work schedule, personal life and earnings in one place.

---

## Target Audience

- Factory workers
- Healthcare workers
- Police officers
- Firefighters
- Logistics employees
- Hospitality workers
- Retail employees
- Anyone working rotating shifts

---

## Core Problems

People working shifts often struggle with:

- remembering upcoming shifts
- calculating salary
- tracking overtime
- tracking vacation days
- managing work-life balance
- organizing personal events around work

---
